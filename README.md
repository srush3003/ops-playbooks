# ops-playbooks

Most teams add AI to look modern. This playbook is about
adding AI where it removes real friction from real work.

Every workflow here was identified and implemented inside
a live agency environment. Each one includes the problem
it solves, the tools that do it, the time it saves, and
what the output actually looks like.

Use what applies. Skip what does not. Adapt everything.

---

## Who this is for

Operators, project coordinators, and technical teams at
agencies and scaling companies who want AI integrated
into their actual workflow, not just their pitch deck.
